A mongrel, mutt or mixed-breed dog is a dog that does not belong to one officially recognized breed and including those that are the result of intentional breeding.


Mongrel dogs can be divided roughly into types:
  • Mixes that show characteristics of two or more breeds.
  • The generic pariah dog, where non-selective breeding has occurred over many generations.
  • Functional breeds, which are purpose-bred dogs selected by their performance at particular tasks.
